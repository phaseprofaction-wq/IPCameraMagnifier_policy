Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how IP Camera Magnifier ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Camera Access: Our app requires camera access to provide the core functionality of capturing and streaming video. We do not store, share, or transmit any images or videos without your explicit action.

Storage Access: We request storage permission only to save photos you intentionally capture. Photos are saved locally on your device and are not automatically shared or uploaded.

2. How We Use Information

We use the camera exclusively for:

  • Live video preview
  • Local streaming within your network
  • Saving photos to your device when you manually capture them

3. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute any personal information or media content to third parties. The streaming feature works only within your local network and does not transmit data to external servers.

4. Network Streaming

The MJPEG streaming feature creates a local web server that operates only within your Wi-Fi network. No video data leaves your local network unless you explicitly configure port forwarding.

5. Permissions

Camera: Required for video capture and streaming

Storage: Required for saving captured photos to your gallery

Network: Required for local streaming functionality

6. Data Security

All video processing occurs locally on your device. We do not operate any external servers that collect or process your data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: phaseprofaction@gmail.com

我们仅将相机用于：

我们不会向第三方共享、出售或分发任何个人信息或媒体内容。流媒体功能仅在您的本地网络内工作，不会将数据传输到外部服务器。

所有视频处理都在您的设备本地进行。我们不运营任何收集或处理您数据的外部服务器。
